Cricket

Equipment: Cricket ball, Cricket bat, Wicket (Stumps, Bails), Various protective equipmen

Team members: 11 players per side (substitutes permitted in some circumstances)

FootBall

Team members: 11 per side (including goalkeeper)
Olympic: Men's since the 1900 Olympics and women's since the 1996 Olympics
Equipment: Football (or soccer ball)
Highest governing body: FIFA
Mixed gender:No, separate competitions

BasketBall

Team members: 5 per side
Highest governing body:  FIBA
Contact: Limited-contact
Venue: Indoor court (mainly) or outdoor court (Streetball)
Mixed gender: Yes, separate competitions

Kabaddi

Contact:  Full
Team members: 7 (per side)
